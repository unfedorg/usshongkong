Guidelines

TL;DR:

  • 1. Respect for All
  • 2. No Solicitation or Commercial Activities

1. Respect for All

We expect all members to foster a welcoming, inclusive, and safe environment for everyone. Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, bullying, or personal attacks of any kind will not be tolerated. Please engage in discussions with an open mind and treat fellow members with kindness and respect, even when differing opinions arise.

2. No Solicitation or Commercial Activities

This community is dedicated to sharing our mutual passion for Star Trek. To maintain a focused and enjoyable environment, please refrain from using the group for commercial purposes or solicitation of any kind. This includes, but is not limited to, selling merchandise, promoting businesses, religious proselytizing, or soliciting for outside organizations. Sending unsolicited private messages to members for these reasons is also strictly prohibited.

Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness any behavior that violates these guidelines, or if you are troubled by someone's actions, please do not hesitate to contact the organizer.
